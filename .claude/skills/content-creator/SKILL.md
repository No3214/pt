# Content Creator Skill

Marketing content for Ela PT brand.

## Brand Voice
- Tone: Professional yet warm, motivating, knowledgeable
- Language: Turkish primary, English secondary
- Audience: Athletes, fitness enthusiasts, volleyball players
- Values: Science-based training, injury prevention, holistic wellness

## Content Types

### Blog Posts (SEO)
- 1500-2500 words, Turkish
- Topics: volleyball training, nutrition, injury prevention, wellness
- Include: header image, subheadings, CTA to programs
- Keywords: voleybol antrenman, sicrama programi, beslenme plani

### Social Media
- Instagram: Before/after transformations, workout clips, nutrition tips
- Reels: Exercise demos with form cues
- Stories: Daily training snippets, client wins
- Hashtags: #voleybol #antrenman #fitness #sporbeslenme

### Email Sequences
- Welcome: Introduction to Ela, philosophy, free resource
- Nurture: Weekly training tips, nutrition advice
- Conversion: Program benefits, testimonials, limited spots

### Landing Page Copy
- Hero: Bold claim + social proof stat
- Benefits: Results-focused, not feature-focused
- Testimonials: Specific metrics (+12cm jump, zero injuries)
- CTA: WhatsApp contact, free consultation

## Writing Rules
- Use t.xxx for all UI text (13 languages)
- Metrics over adjectives ("+12cm" not "significant improvement")
- Active voice, short sentences
- Turkish: Sade, anlasilir, motive edici
